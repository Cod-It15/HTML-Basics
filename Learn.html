Water
Water is an inorganic compound with the chemical formula H2O, composed of hydrogen and oxygen. It exists in three states: liquid, solid (ice), and gas (steam or vapor). Water is essential for all known forms of life and covers about 71% of the Earth's surface, forming lakes, oceans, rivers, and rainfall.
Coffee
Coffee is a beverage brewed from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of the tropical evergreen coffee plant of African origin.
Cappacuino
A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink made with a balanced combination of espresso, steamed milk, and a layer of milk foam on top. It's known for its rich flavor and creamy texture.
Soft Drink
Soft drinks are sweetened beverages that typically contain carbonated water, a sweetener, and flavorings.
Smoothies
Smoothies are blended beverages made from fruits, vegetables, and often other ingredients like yogurt or protein powders. They are popular for their nutritional benefits and versatility, serving as natural energy drinks or filling breakfasts.
